Gidole

Gidole is a beautiful mountain town in southern Ethiopia where I grew up.
It's my first typeface with feedback/help from typophile and r/typography.

Design

The design is inspired by the classic DIN but with wider apertures, narrower glyphs and a more minimal appearance than other recent iterations.

Open Source

100% free OFL license for private + commercial use.
I'm using Fontforge and the source is on Github.
