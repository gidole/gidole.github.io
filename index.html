Gidole

Gidole is a beautiful mountain town in southern Ethiopia where I spent 4 childhood years and gidolinya is the language spoken by the people living there - hence the names.

Design

The typefaces are inspired by the classic DIN #1451. The letters are drawn with Euler spirals using circles as guidelines for a minimal look with the smoothest curves and curve to tangent transitions are as smooth as possible.

Guidelines

I recommend you increase and decrease the tracking for body text and big headlines, respectively.

Open Source + 100% Free

Help me by reporting issues or making improvements by forking and creating pull requests. FontForge is a free font editor and I've also saved UFO versions.
